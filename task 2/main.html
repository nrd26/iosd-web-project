Manipal is a locality of Udupi, located 5 kilometres from the centre of the city in Udupi district of the state of Karnataka in India and is administered by the Udupi City Municipality. It is located in the part of Coastal Karnataka, 62.8 kms. north of Mangalore and 8 km east of the Arabian Sea.

Manipal is home to the Manipal Academy of Higher Education and lures students from different places. This town is famous for a number of attractions as well. End Point Park, Planetarium, Manipal Museum of Anatomy and Pathology, Hasta Shilpa Heritage Village, Manipal Lake and more are must-visit places.
